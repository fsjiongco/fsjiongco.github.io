Favorite GE so far

Through my first two years of being a UPLB student, Ethics 1 is still the GE class I am most fond of. Through this subject, I was able to think a lot more critically about a variety of topics. Ma'am Cassandra Teodosio's discussions kept me interested over the year. I was in awe at how easily she explained all of the concepts effortlessly. In addition, the short assessments were just right for stimulating my mind. With the aid of this subject, I was shocked at how I was able to condense a large number of thoughts into a sentence or two.

Favorite CMSC subject so far

My favorite CMSC subject so far is CMSC 150: Numerical and Symbolic Computations. Before entering UPLB as a computer science major, I had very limited exposure to programming. Therefore, the frustration and the satisfaction I felt, the eureka moments, and the trial and error I experienced amid this course showed me how much I have grown and improved as a programmer. Besides, I've always enjoyed a good challenge, so the exercises in this course pushed me to my limits and allowed me to surpass my own standards.

Favorite programming language so far
